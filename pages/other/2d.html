New Follower Animation

I was applying to a company and as an art test they asked me to create an animation like those that are used on Twitch. I made the follower one first and decided it wasn't of high enough quality so then I created the supply drop donation animation. I first drafted out on paper what kind of animation to do. The first follower was both a test and a practice piece because I haven't done much animation in a while. So I wanted practice following the rules of animation. After coming up with the idea of a Fortnite game themed animation. The supply crate seemed like the best plan. Then I wanted to have the name pop out like it was a weapon in the game. I spent the most of my time on this animation in creating the supply crate asset. The hardest part of this was with not having a lot of reference images since the game is very new.
