Toon Guy Idle

Toon Guy Idle Animation by robotboy1015 on Sketchfab

I have created these animations to serve as idle animations. I made sure to use real people as reference then I would go home and practice the same thing. This allowed me to really know the animation. I also recorded myself doing these animations for reference while I made the animations. The hardest part of animating the toon guy was making it seem like he was listening to music without making every action on beat because then it does look as real. I made both of these characters in Adobe Fuse.
