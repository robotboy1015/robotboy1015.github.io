Environment Art

Post Apocalyptic Environment

I modeled the majority of the assets in this scene and textured every asset. The models I was given to work with are the pipe, the cinderblocks, and the X-plate pieces I used to make the generator room. I textured everything in Substance Painter. The purpose of the project was to make modular assets to create an environment. I was very effcient in reusing models for different things. One of the very interesting parts of the project was the I-beams that I modeled and created a UE blueprint for that allowed me to create varying length I beams to support the scaffolding up the side of the cooling tower.

Modular Wall

Modular Wall 1 (Post Apocolypse Wall) by robotboy1015 on Sketchfab

For this project I needed a few walls to help build an environment and so in the style of post apocalyptic world I made a few assets that are meant to look like they were built after everything else went downhill. They are dirty and worn down after a few years of wear and tear and zombies or monsters or what have you. This is one of my first texturing projects.
